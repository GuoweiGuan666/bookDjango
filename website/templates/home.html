About Us

The boolean book club meets on mondays from 2- 5 in the Terance Lab at UBC. Our meetings are centered around genre themes that change every month. Our club officers also support members who wish to write their own books and will meet you to proofread and offer feedback on your writing. If you join you can also take part in our competition to see who can read 1000 pages first each month. You can see the top three members for this month below!
